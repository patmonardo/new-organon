## Historical-Philosophical Context

### Jasche's Logic and Fichte's Refutation

**Jasche reworked the Logic in light of Fichte's Nondual Kantianism**:
- Jasche admits that **Fichte has refuted Kantian Logic**
- But Jasche **defends Kant's metaphysics**

This is a crucial historical moment:
- **Fichte refuted Kantian Logic** (the logical structure)
- **Kant's metaphysics defended** (the metaphysical content)
- **Jasche Logic** = Attempt to reconcile/rework Logic in light of Fichte's Nondual Kantianism
